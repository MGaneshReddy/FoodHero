About us

" Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity. How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world. – Anne Frank "

Food Hero is an organisation which aims to redistribute excess food from weddings, parties, events to those who are hungry.

The idea took root in Hyderabad, where we started with 2 Shopper Bags to collect the surplus food in our community and deliver to the homeless

We took an oath on that day to stop food waste and satisfy the hunger of many

We aim to bring benefits across the spectrum of society and help provide access to food to those who don’t have adequate means to access it.
